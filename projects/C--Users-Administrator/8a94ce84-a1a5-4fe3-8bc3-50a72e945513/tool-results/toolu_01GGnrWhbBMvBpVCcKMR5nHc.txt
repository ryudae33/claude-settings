   555тЖТ5.2.4 Action of the escaper 
   556тЖТThe escaper rotates 90┬░ clockwise, stops for about 0.6 seconds, and rotates again 90┬░ clockwise. 
   557тЖТWhen a screw is caught in the escaper and the notch position is altered, the escaper 
   558тЖТautomatically rotates to the left, as a reference point run, in order to adjust the notch position 
   559тЖТand then returns to the right rotation. 
   560тЖТThis machine continues its operation when no screw is found at the screw pick up spot. The 
   561тЖТmachine continues operating with a screw at the pickup spot but will stop, after a certain lapse 
   562тЖТof time, if the screw is not picked up. After the screw is picked up, the machine starts operating 
   563тЖТagain. This time lapse can be varied by adjusting the timer. 
   564тЖТWhen no screw is found at the pickup site after a certain lapse of time, the rail vibration 
   565тЖТincreases. (The vibration sound, also, increases however, this is not a problem.) If no screw is 
   566тЖТstill not found at the pick up site, then the machine stops operating. At this time the escaper 
   567тЖТkeeps rotating. When you want to start operation again, turn the power switch OFF and ON 
   568тЖТagain.

   641тЖТ5.4 Checking and adjusting the rail vibration 
   642тЖТThe amplitude and frequency of the rail vibration can be adjusted. 
   643тЖТThe vibration has been adjusted at the factory for screws that correspond with the rail. 
   644тЖТPut some screws into the rail and turn the power on. If they are delivered smoothly, there 
   645тЖТis no need for adjustment. 
   646тЖТThe screw transport feed differs depending on screw type. 
   647тЖТFor screws with a low transport speed, or screws that easily jump out, an adjustment is 
   648тЖТnecessary. 
   649тЖТ1. Turn the frequency adjusting knob (first hole at the top in the back of the machine) using 
   650тЖТthe accompanying screwdriver. 
   651тЖТFind the frequency at which the rail vibrates the most. 
   652тЖТ2. Turn the amplitude adjusting knob (second hole from the top) and find the amplitude for 
   653тЖТwhich screws are delivered smoothly. 
   654тЖТ 
   655тЖТIf the vibration is adjusted to a too large a value to increase the transport speed, screws may 
   656тЖТjump from the rail and fall into the machine from the clearance, failing to unload screws 
   657тЖТnormally. 
   658тЖТAdjust the vibration to a proper value that matches the loaded screws. 
   659тЖТWith the accompanying screwdriver, turn the knob without using excessive force. 
   660тЖТWhen no screws are scooped onto the rail for a certain period of time, the rail vibration 
   661тЖТincreases. If, still, no screws are scooped on to the rail, the machine stops operating.

  1271тЖТMiscellaneous 
  1272тЖТ9.1 Overload Protective Circuit 
  1273тЖТThis machine is provided with an overload protective circuit. Usually, the driving motor 
  1274тЖТrotates forward (normal rotation) to feed loaded screws to the escaper continuously, thus 
  1275тЖТpicking up screws one after another. However, if an overload is applied to the driving section, 
  1276тЖТthe driving motor rotates backward for a certain time and then returns to the normal 
  1277тЖТrotation. When the cause of the overload is removed at the reverse rotation, the driving 
  1278тЖТmotor returns to the ordinary normal rotation and can pick up screws. 
  1279тЖТIf the cause of the overload is not removed during the reverse rotation, the driving motor 
  1280тЖТrepeats the sequence of reverse rotation - normal rotation - reverse rotation - normal rotation 
  1281тЖТfor a certain time to shut off the power to the driving motor. At this time, the escaper 
  1282тЖТoperation is not stopped. When the power to the driving motor is shut off, turn off the power 
  1283тЖТswitch and remove the cause of the over- load. For example, when too many screws are put 
  1284тЖТinto the scooping chamber, reduce the quantity of loaded screws to a proper level. If any 
  1285тЖТscrew is caught by the driving section, remove it. 
  1286тЖТAfter removing the cause of the overload, turn on the power switch to operate this machine. 
  1287тЖТ(Power reset)
